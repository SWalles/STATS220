Introduction

I am not a very avid user of social media, but I recognise that this is not the case for everyone. I therefore decided to create a form to investigate peoples social media habits. I was interested to know how much time people spent on social media and what sorts of social media they used. I also recorded some information about the kind of people who answered the form, like age, interest, and personality type. I made sure to keep respondents anonymous.

I was careful about using response validation. For numeric answers I specified specific ranges. For ages I only allowed values from 12 to 120. For the number of hours spent on social media I only allowed values between 0 and 168 as there are only 168 hours in a week. I also asked people to describe their sentiment towards social media. I validated this question using a regular expression (^[a-zA-Z]{1,16}$) that only allowed one word of up to 16 characters. On reflection I realised I also should not allowed upper-case letters. All other questions had fixed options for participants to choose from.

For analysing changes between weeks, I could calculate and compare the mean hours spent on social media for each week. I also asked people to rate their feelings toward social media on a 1 to 5 scale (negative to positive). This could allow me to track how people feel about social media over time. I can also track popularity across some social media platforms using the data from question 5.

Reflection

Most of my participants were around 15 to 20 years old. They had a variety of interests and I have classified most of them as playful.

A lot of my participants spend upwards of 10 hours a week on social media. Youtube, Instagram, and Facebook are the most popular social media platforms among my participants and they also seem too have a preference for Video Sharing / Streaming platforms.

Most of my participants have rated their feelings toward social media as 3 or better on a scale from 1 to 5. When asked to describe their sentiment towards social media I got a wide variety of responses from entertaining and informative to addicting and resentment.

Creativity

I believe that I mostly demonstrated creativity with regards to the last question on my form. I asked people to describe in one word how they generally felt about social media. I used a regular expression to limit answers to one word of up to 16 characters. I then had to find a way to display this information in my report. After doing some research I settled on using the ggwordcloud package to create a word cloud and colour words according to their popularity.

I also applied knowledge I have gained from other courses like STATS 380 to improve the presentation of the data in the analytics section.
